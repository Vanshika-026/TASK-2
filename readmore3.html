-Parul Jain :Yoga Scholar

Parul Jain is an internationally certified yoga teacher, practicing and teaching yoga for almost a decade now. She has completed 200 Hours of Teacher Training Courses and 85 Hours of Prenatal Postnatal Teacher Training Courses from institutes affiliated with Yoga Alliance, USA. She has done Masters in Yoga. She has undergone numerous certificate courses on varied yoga subjects. She is specifically trained in Hatha yoga, Ashtanga yoga, Vinyasa flows and yoga using props.

She has been conducting sessions for Indian as well as international clients and helping them in addressing their health concerns, like weight loss, thyroid, back pain, joint pain, women's health, general fitness, etc. One of her key abilities is her customized approach depending upon the needs of her students. She likes to stay invested in her student's yoga journey.

'Not investing in health means losing wealth' is a simple mantra that Parul Jain lives by. She wishes to inspire everyone around her to practice yoga. Her goal is to spread awareness about this beautiful practice and help people maintain good health. Her one piece of advice to anyone starting their yogic journey is 'to allow oneself to be a beginner and just grow with the flow'.
